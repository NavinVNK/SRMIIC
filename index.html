Workspace
SIIC has two spaces one is FAB LAB located next to SRM Clock tower, which is a fabrication laboratory equipped with digital fabrication facilities, tools, and machinery. And another one is SIIC office space located at 5th Floor, Basic Engineering Lab (Aka BEL Building) which has private office spaces, coworking space, training room, and a meeting room. All the workspaces are biometrically secured and cannot be accessed by unauthorized personnel.

Legal Assistance
Having the correct legal structure is pivotal to the establishment and growth of a startup. We provide you with the legal assistance you require to begin a commercial venture.
Design Branding
With the startup space growing and evolving every day, creating a brand value and a brand identity is something that has become a critical factor in staying ahead of the competition. That is something we understand and we can provide you with the assistance you need to stay ahead. From creating your logo to starting a social media campaign, we can help you grow your brand value.
Patent Registration
We know that your ideas are new and unique, and we can help you protect your innovative ideas. Patent registration might be a new process to a lot of student innovators. We will help you register a patent for your idea, protect it, and reserve it as yours.
Industry Advisors
Over the last half-decade, we have connected to a plethora of successful entrepreneurs, businessmen, industry specialists, investors, and organizations that share our vision. We can help you come in contact with them and they can provide you with industry-level knowledge, information, experience, and resources that can help you in countless ways.
Cloud Credits
At SIIC, we believe in keeping pace with technological advancements. Today, most workflows are dependent on a cloud-based service such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud Platform(GCP), etc. However, making use of cloud services for your startup will cost you a lot which is why we provide you with a certain number of cloud credits to enable your cloud-based workflows and needs.
Technical Mentorship
If you face any technical predicaments or need some mentoring to continue the development and prototyping of your idea, SIIC has a fantastic network of mentors. They are very experienced in multiple fields and possess a vast spectrum of technical knowledge and resources and are willing to help you navigate your challenges. You can tap into this network whenever you feel the need.
Fabrication Facility
Our fabrication facility, FABLAB is a 20,000 sq. feet co-working space equipped with top of the line fabricating tools. These tools include machines such as laser cutters, 3D Printers, welding machines, lathes, and much more, as well as multiple high-performance workstations equipped with NVIDIA Quadro GPU's for all your design and testing needs. Further, it also includes an air-conditioned meeting room for collaborative work and brainstorming sessions. If you need assistance, technical assistants are always present at FABLAB for you.
Market Validation
Market validation is a critical process in the product development cycle. It can be difficult to find the target market that will be interested in your product or concept. This is where we can help you. We can help you introduce your product to the target audience and help you analyze the feedback that you receive most optimally.
Co-Working Space
Today, collaboration and the exchange of ideas is the fabric of the startup ecosystem. To facilitate this, we have a state-of-the-art co-working space. This space is retrofitted with air conditioning, great lighting, fast wifi connection as well as a space to host networking events with high-end mics and sound systems. All students who are a part of our ecosystem get access to this co-working space and its facilities.
Alumni Connect
SRM IST has a very successful alumni in the field of entrepreneurship and business. Every year, a large number of alumni approach us in the context of helping, mentoring, or investing in you, the student innovators. We will aid you in contacting them or approaching them. They have valuable knowledge, experience, and resources that can be extremely useful for your growth.
